Maria Bello Health Update: Is She Sick? What Happened To Her?

Maria Bello has bipolar disorder but has learned to cope with the disease. She was identified as having bipolar disorder when she was twenty-eight years old.

Since taking medication, she views her illness as a gift, allowing her to experience the full range of human emotion and sensation, from the lowest point to the highest.

Maria has become more humble because of the diagnosis. 

She firmly believes that both physical and mental well-being are interrelated.

According to her, some of the sickest individuals may recover due to a positive view.

Before that, the actress was admitted to the hospital in 2013 after getting a parasite infection in Haiti.

She participated in a charitable mission while confined to bed for several months.

Currently, the NCIS actress is doing fine and is busy making movies and shows she enjoys. 

The actress typically drinks green juice first in the morning since it gives her more energy than coffee.

Maria combines kale, cucumber, celery, apple, and jalapeno pepper for a sweet and salty flavor.

She claims that her finest healthy habit is visiting a nutritionist. 

Maria uses the same brand of vitamins and supplements she has been using for more than 20 years daily. She can tell the difference if she misses.

Moreover, her worst habit is smoking, but she occasionally considers giving it up.

Maria Bello’s Personal Life 

Maira Bello was born on April 18, 1967, in Norristown, Pennsylvania, to her mother, Kathy, a school nurse and teacher, and father, Joe Bello, a contractor.

Her mother is a Polish American, while her father is an Italian American with roots in Montella, Italy.

Besides, the actress was raised in a working-class Catholic home.

She completed high school at Archbishop John Carroll High School in Radnor, Pennsylvania.

At Villanova University, Maria studied political science as her major.

Her dream was to be a lawyer, but after taking an acting class during her senior year, she became interested in acting. 

After graduation, she developed her acting abilities in several New York theatrical shows. 

Maria and her ex-boyfriend Dan McDermott have a son named Jackson.

In 2013, she came out of the closet and identified as bisexual. 

In her book, she discussed having a same-sex relationship with Clare Munn, her then-girlfriend.

Bello and chef Dominique Crenn made their first public appearance as a couple in February 2020 at the 92nd Academy Awards.

She revealed they had been engaged on December 29, 2019, while on vacation in Paris, France.
