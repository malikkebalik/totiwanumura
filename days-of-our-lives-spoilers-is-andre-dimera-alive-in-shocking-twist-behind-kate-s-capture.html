Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Is Andre DiMera Alive in Shocking Twist Behind Kates Capture?

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that someone is holding Kate Roberts Brady (Lauren Koslow) captive on that fishing boat, but who’s to blame? There are some interesting clues to ponder as we gear up for the show’s big reveal.

Although Louise Sorel is returning as Vivian Alamain, it’s too early for her to start airing just yet.

Due to the show’s filming schedule, it should be a few more months before Vivian is back on our screens.

With that in mind, Kate’s captor must be someone else. Days spoilers tease that it’s a villain from DOOL history, so it’s someone who’ll be easily recognizable to fans.

This mystery villain has an equally mysterious agenda involving Kate, but we could certainly buy the idea of Andre kidnapping his former wife.

Although Andre is believed to be deceased, so was Bo Brady (Peter Reckell) until a twist brought him back to life.

That’s been happening a lot lately, so maybe Andre will be the latest character to return from the dead and get caught up in a new storyline.

We should mention that May preview info also points to a mystery DiMera getting caught up in some family drama.

Days spoilers say Megan Hathaway (Miranda Wilson) will plot with Kristen DiMera (Stacy Haiduk) to get out of Statesville and score a proper place in the DiMera clan.

While Kristen and Megan are scheming, there’ll be another DiMera who enters the mix and shakes things up.

Andre could be the DiMera that Days writers have in mind, especially since it’d be easy for Thaao Penghlis (Tony DiMera) to do double duty.

Chad DiMera (Billy Flynn) will play a major role in rescuing Kate along with Harris Michaels (Steve Burton) and Steve Johnson (Stephen Nichols), so could Chad come face to face with his “dead” brother and ultimately reunite with Andre?

Regardless, Kate indeed has a secret captor – and we know a secret DiMera will become part of all the DiMera family action at some point, too.

Could all of that add up to Andre DiMera returning from the dead? Will Andre offer updates on his grand plans for Kate and deliver some shocking news?

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Andre’s return could cause chaos, so stay tuned to see if he’s alive and holding Kate hostage.
