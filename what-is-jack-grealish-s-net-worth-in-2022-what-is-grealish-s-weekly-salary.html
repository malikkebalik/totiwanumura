What is Jack Grealishs net worth in 2022? What is Grealishs weekly salary?

In recent years, Jack Grealish has been one of the most exciting midfield talents to emerge in England. His outstanding performances for Aston Villa earned him a lucrative summer 2021 move to Manchester City.

He was instrumental in Villa’s ascent to the Premier League and subsequent survival in English football’s top tier. He grew up an Aston Villa supporter and was strongly connected to the club.

Still, a player of his skill deserves the opportunity to win a trophy at some point in their career, and a move to the Etihad Stadium was an opportunity he couldn’t pass up.

Although his performances under Pep Guardiola have not yet lived up to the lofty expectations placed on him following his move to Manchester, the English international has shown flashes of why he is so highly regarded in his homeland.

Jack Grealish’s net worth

Grealish’s net worth is estimated to be around 20 million euros as of February 2022. He joined Manchester City from Aston Villa for 117.7 million euros, marking him the Premier League’s most expensive player.

How much money does Grealish earn?

After joining City for such a high cost, Grealish was certainly rewarded with a lucrative contract. His weekly wage at the club is 360,000 euros, second only to Kevin de Bruyne (420,000 euros).

What sponsorship deals does Grealish have?

Grealish also has sponsorship deals with Nike and Electronic Arts, which add to his huge pay at Manchester City.

His present deal with Nike is set to expire after the season. Still, the American business is prepared to offer him an even bigger deal in the future. However, other major brands such as Adidas, Puma, and Under Armour are also planning offers for the England ace.
