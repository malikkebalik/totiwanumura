All About Washington University Professor Seth Crosby

Seth Crosby (age 63, born in 1960) is a professor fired by Washington University for speaking against Hamas on Twitter. Some people who supported Palestine didn’t agree with what Crosby said, and they wanted him to lose his job.

They took pictures of what Crosby wrote on Twitter and shared them online, hoping the university would do something about it. Washington University saw these posts and decided to let Crosby go.

It is worth noting that in the past, some students had expressed the view that Hamas’ actions against Israel were a necessary measure. However, no disciplinary action was taken against these students. This raises questions about the university’s handling of freedom of speech in cases where differing viewpoints are expressed.
