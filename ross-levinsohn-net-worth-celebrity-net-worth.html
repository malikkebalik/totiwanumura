Ross Levinsohn net worth: Ross Levinsohn is an American business executive who has a net worth of $40 million dollars. In 2011 alone Ross earned a salary of $12 million. Born in Tenafly, New Jersey, Ross Levinsohn earned his Bachelor's from American University, where he studied Broadcast Communications. He began working in marketing, management, and advertising after school, for such companies as Lapin and Rose Communications, ProServe, and Saatchi & Saatchi. He then became an executive producer at CBS Sportsline, and then moved on to serving as an upper level executive and eventual Vice President of Alta Vista in the late 90s.

He launched his own digital media and communications group, called Velocity Interactive Group, in late 2007. The company, which is also run by Jonathan Miller and Kayur Patel, invests in a wide variety of media and technology concerns worldwide. He served as Head of Global Media, among other positions, for Yahoo! beginning in 2010, and served as CEO from May 2012 to July 2012.
