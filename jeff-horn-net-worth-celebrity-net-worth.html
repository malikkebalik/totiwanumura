What is Jeff Horn's Net Worth?

Jeff Horn is an Australian professional boxer who has a net worth of $6 milllion. Jeff Horn was born in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia in February 1988. He has competed as a welterweight and a middleweight. Horn represented Australia as an amateur at the 2012 Olympics. He made his professional boxing debut in March 2013 with a win over Jody Allen. In September 2013 Horn won the vacant Australian welterweight title by defeating Samuel Colomban. In March 2014 he beat Rivan Cesaire to win the vacant WBO Oriental welterweight title.

Jeff Horn won the vacant PABA welterweight title by beating Fernando Ferreira da Silva in July 2014. In June 2015 he defeated Richmond Djarbeng to win the WBA Pan African welterweight title. Horn won the IBF Inter-Continental welterweight title with a win over Viktor Plotnikov in August 2015. He defeated Alfredo Rodolfo Blanco in October 2015 to win the vacant WBA Oceania interim welterweight title. Horn won the vacant WBO Inter-Continental welterweight title over Randall Bailey in April 2016. In July 2017 he beat Manny Pacquiao to win the WBO welterweight title. He lost the title to Terence Crawford in his first loss in June 2018 before winning the WBO Oriental and vacant Oceania middleweight titles with a win over Anthony Mundine in November to bring his record to 19-1-1.
