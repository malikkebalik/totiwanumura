Lily Gladstone Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Lily Gladstone was born on August 2, 1986 in Kalispell, Montana. She attended the University of Montana and studied acting and directing. In 2012, she made her acting debut in the French drama film Jimmy P: Psychotherapy of a Plains Indian. After that, she had a role in the Hollywood movie Winter in the Blood.

Gladstone’s breakout role was as Jamie in the drama film Certain Women, winning awards at the Los Angeles Film Critics Association and Boston Society of Film Critics for the Best Supporting Actress category. She also received a nomination at the Independent Spirit Award. She subsequently had performances in the films Buster’s Mal Heart, Walking Out, and First Cow.
