Maldivas Actress Sheron Menezzes Bio | Wiki

Sheron Menezzes (born on November 26, 1983) is a 38 years old fantastic actress from the Rio Grande do Sul. She is popularly known for her presence in the TV industry. Many remember her as  Gisele Cavalcanti, the character she played in the 2019 hit TV drama “A Life Worth Living.” Another show which gave Menezzes unmatched popularity is the 2017 show “Novo Mundo,” a romantic drama where she played the role of Diara.

In 2022, Sheron crossed all boundaries of success after appearing as Rayssa in a mystery & comedy-drama “Maldivas,” but apart from that, she is working day and night on a movie titled “Carga Máxima,” which could be released at the end of 2022.

Early Life

On November 26, 1983, Sheron Menezzes took birth to her father, Haroldo Menezes, and mother, Veralindá Ménezes, in Porto Alegre, the Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Sheron is one of the four kids of her parents as she has three siblings named Draiton, Sol, and Drayson. The actress spent her entire childhood in Porto Alegre.

After completion of schooling, she did Bachelor’s in Fine Arts (BFA) from a renowned university. Later she connected with a reputed talent management agency and searched for acting roles. In 2002, she received her first acting job for a show called “Terra Speranza.” The serial ran from 2002 to 2003, and Menezzes appeared in this project as Julia.

Career

A year later, Sheron got picked to play Lara in the rom-com “Celebrity.” In a true sense, this was the show that helped Menezzes earn the status of a celebrity. After “Celebrity,” this Brazilian actress’ career leaped, and she played significant roles in different shows like “Like a Wave,” “Belíssima,” “The Cleaning Lady,” “Watercolors of Love,” “Além do Horizonte,” etc.

Besides shows, Sheron began working as a movie actress in 2003, and the first film she did was “The Man Who Copied” as Guria Esperta. Although Mancilha’s film career started with a short movie in 2012, she worked in a star-studded movie, “The Inventor of Dreams.”

As Mancilha’s popularity increased, famous brands started approaching her for the publicity of their products. Currently, she is the Brazilian brand ambassador of the luxury brand Lancôme Paris.

Some of the award-winning shows in which Sheron worked were “Side by Side,” “Ambitious Women,” “Lady Revolution,” “Novo Mundo,” and “A Life Worth Living.” Currently, she resides in a  luxurious mention with her family, and as per sources, her net worth is $1.68 million.

Love Life & Kid

The Brazilian actress Sheron Menezzes is currently married to a musician named Saulo Camilo. They both were in a relationship for a significant time. The couple even has a son named Benjamin. After taking their time, in 2022, both stars decided to tie the knot, and the wedding happened near a beach in Brazil.

Many famous actors and singers were present on their wedding day. Menezzes also shared a series of photos on her Instagram account (@sheronmenezzes). You must follow her social media handles if you want to stay connected with your favorite Brazilian actress.
