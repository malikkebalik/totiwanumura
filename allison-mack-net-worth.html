Allison Mack is an American actress whose acting career started when she was little in 1989. In the early 1990s, she had roles in the movies Night Eyes 3 and Camp Nowhere. She also had roles in several TV movies. Her last movie in the 1990s was in Honey, We Shrunk Ourselves. She came to mainstream prominence in the 2000s for her role as Chloe Sullivan on the hit series Smallville. She was on the show until its end in 2011. Afterwards, she appeared as a recurring character on Wilfred. She had guest roles on The Following and American Odyssey.
