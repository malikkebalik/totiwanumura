What is Ursula Engelhorn's Net Worth?

Ursula Engelhorn is a German businesswoman who has a net worth of$3.7 billion. Traudl Engelhorn is part of the German Engelhorn family whose patriarch Fredrich Engelhorn founded the chemical company BASF in 1865. Ursula is the widow of Christof Engelhorn whose brother Curt ran the family business Boehringer Mannheim for over 30 years and expanded into biochemical and pharmaceutical operations. The company was sold to Roche for $11 billion in 1997.

Christof Engelhorn owned a 22.3% stake in Corange Ltd. which is the family holding company and nettled almost $2.5 billion. The fortune was split between Ursula, her daughter Vera, and her two grandchildren from her deceased son Stefan. Ursula Engelhorn lives in Lucerne, Switzerland.
