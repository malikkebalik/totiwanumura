Kahla Jacoby Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth, Wiki

Kahla Jacoby is a former figure champion. She’s the wife of famous American mixed martial artist Dustin Jacoby. She’s been married to Dustin since 2013. The couple is currently based in Colorado. She is currently an associate financial representative at North-western Mutual and a lead cause entrepreneur at ONEHOPE Wine.

Early Life

Kahla Jacoby is from Beardstown, Illinois, and seems to be in her thirties. Her exact date of birth is not known but she celebrates her birthday on the 30th of August according to her social media posts. Kahla went to Beardstown senior high school in Illinois. Growing up Kahla was very invested in sports. She was in the Culver- Stockton volleyball team Wildcats.

Kahla Jacoby Career

She is a former winner of a figure competition and was quite athletic while growing up. Kahla Jacoby used to play volleyball in her college days. Kahla Jacoby has always supported her husband in his career. She maintained the home front while Dustin competed as a kickboxer and as an MMA fighter. Due to her constant motivation and dedication, he was ranked the No. 8 middleweight kickboxer in the world by Combat Press.
