Folly Beach Samantha Hutchinson Obituary: Drunk Driver Killed The Bride

A tragic incident occurred on Friday evening in Folly Beach, South Carolina, where a newlywed bride lost her life, and her husband was left in critical condition.

Samantha Miller, aged 34, and Aric Hutchinson, aged 36, enjoyed their wedding celebration when the unfortunate event occurred.

The couple and two others left the reception venue in a golf cart, only to be rear-ended by a speeding drunk driver.

The collision’s force was so powerful that it caused the golf cart to flip over, repeatedly rolling for a distance of 100 yards.

Unfortunately, Hutchinson succumbed to her injuries on the spot while her husband was left in a critical state.

The incident is a heartbreaking reminder of the devastating consequences of driving under the influence and highlights the need for increased vigilance and responsibility while behind the wheel.

Annette Hutchinson, Mother-in-Law of Samantha Hutchinson, Launches GoFundMe Campaign to Support Family

In the wake of the tragic accident that left Samantha Hutchinson dead and her husband, Aric, critically injured, Annette Hutchinson, Aric’s mother, took the initiative to launch a GoFundMe page to help cover her son’s medical expenses.

Per the information provided on the page, Aric has already undergone one reconstructive surgery, with plans for another.

He sustained several broken bones and a brain injury, necessitating his hospitalization in critical condition.

Moreover, Aric’s brother-in-law, Ben Garrett, and his nephew Brogan, who were also in the golf cart during the accident, suffered severe injuries.

Fortunately, Brogan has been discharged from the hospital and is currently recovering from his injuries, as per the GoFundMe updates.

The fundraising Campaign launched by Annette Hutchinson has already surpassed its initial goal of USD 100,000. It has raised USD 382,699 to support the Hutchinson family during this difficult time.
