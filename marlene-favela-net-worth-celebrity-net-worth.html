What is Marlene Favela's Net Worth?

Marlene Favela is a Mexican actress who has a net worth of $5 million. She is best known to American audiences for her role as Esmeralda/Sister Eva in the television series Ugly Betty.

Marlene Favela was born in Santiago Papasquiaro, Durango, Mexico in August 1976. Her debut role came in the TV series Maria Jose in 1995. Her best known role came as Esmeralda and Sister Eva in the TV series Ugly Betty in 2006.

One of her best known Mexican roles came as Esmeralda in the series Zorro: La Espada y La Rosa in 2007. She has also starred in the TV series Maria Isabel, Mujeres enganadas, Port u amor, Infierno en el Paraiso, DKDA: Suenos de juventud, La casa en la playa, Carita de angel, The Outsider, Navidad sin fin, Entre el amor y el odio, Gata salvaje, Velo de novia, Rubi, Contra viento y marea, Amor sin maquillaje, Los Herederos del Monte, Corazon Apasionado, El Rostro de la Venganza, and El Senor de los Cielos. She also starred as Elena in the movie Playball in 2008 and in the video Species: The Awakening in 2007 as Azura. Favela won a Premios ACE Award for Best TV Scene Actress in 2007.
