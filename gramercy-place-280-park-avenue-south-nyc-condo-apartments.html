Description of Gramercy Place at 280 Park Avenue South

Gramercy Place is a full-service condo located at 280 Park Avenue South, on the corner of East 22nd Street. Staff and amenities include full-time doormen, concierge, full-time live-in resident manager, fitness center, rooftop pool, hot tub, sauna, steam room, new laundry room, basement storage, bike storage, and planted and furnished roof decks with amazing views. Union Square Park, Madison Square Park, the Flatiron District, Eataly, Whole Foods, and acclaimed restaurants and shopping are close by.
