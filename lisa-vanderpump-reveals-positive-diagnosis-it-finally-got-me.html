Lisa Vanderpump Reveals Positive Diagnosis: 'It Finally Got Me'

Lisa Vanderpump hasn’t had an easy couple of years.

Since Vanderpump Rules lost many of its heavy hitters this season, it is unclear what the future holds for Lisa and her brands.

Now, Lisa has revealed that she tested positive for COVID-19.
