Cruise to Luganville, Vanuatu | Celebrity Cruises Canada

Luganville is the second-largest town on Vanuatu, one of over 300 islands located in Melanesia. Its population is extremely diverse, with over 100 different languages, a multitude of dialects, and a South Pacific island heritage that spans over 3,000 years of immigration. On Luganville's main street, (which is unusually wide, thanks to a WWII general's insistence that it should accommodate four trucks abreast) you'll find reasonably-priced restaurants along with interesting general stores, shops, and boutiques.
