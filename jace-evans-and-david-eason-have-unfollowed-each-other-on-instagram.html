Jace Evans and David Eason Have Unfollowed Each Other On Instagram

Credit: MTV

It does not look like things are resolving themselves for Jenelle Evans and her son. There are new claims that David Eason and Jace Evans have now gone public with their falling out by unfollowing each other on Instagram.

There’s another sign that things are not okay in Jenelle Evans’s family and this time, it’s online.

Her husband David Eason and her son Jace have unfollowed each other on Instagram.

The news comes after a report from TMZ that Jenelle and David might be facing charges in the CPS case about the 14-year-old.

There has been an ongoing series of issues from The Land recently. On September 29, Jace was reported missing for a third time. It was after this that police began treating the case as an investigation into possible neglect.

There are also claims of multiple forms of evidence that David assaulted the Jace. It’s no surprise that they have reportedly cut ties on social media.

According to TMZ, the teenage boy escaped through his window and went out into the woods that surround the family’s home.

Jenelle told law enforcement that her son left the house without a phone. The police showed up to their home around 8:00 p.m., TMZ noted.

Nearly 24 hours later, Jenelle took to social media to alert followers that Jace was safely found. He was later taken from the Easons’ custody and is now back with his grandmother Barbara.

A user on Reddit pointed out that while Jace and his stepdad previously followed the other’s Instagram accounts, that is no longer the case.

Jace and David have now appeared to officially unfollow each other.

In a video posted to Instagram at the end of September, the former Teen Mom 2 star asked fans for privacy.
